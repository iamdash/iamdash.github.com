Highlighted Work

GTS Scouting App

Client:
Wink Nudge/Tactica

A hybrid mobile app developed for and distributed by football clubs at all levels (including the Premier League) to their talent scouts to allow them to create and submit reports on players they are assigned to via a bespoke AngularJS-based management system which sat on top of a NodeJS/Express API.

Tesco Malls Tablet App

Client:
Wink Nudge/Innesco

A hybrid tablet app built on top of a Wordpress backend for use at exhibitions. The app includes photo galleries, a number of case studies and an interactive map that allows the user to view details on over 400 locations across central and Eastern Europe.

In-Flight web portals

Client:
Black Swan / Panasonic Avionics

As a senior developer for Black Swan, I was involved in the development of in-flight, JavaScript-based web portals. These portals provided fully interactive experiences for airline passengers which they could access via their laptops, tablets or mobile phones over in-flight wi-fi.
